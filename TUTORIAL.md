# HopeVoice Hub - User Tutorial & Guide

## 🏠 Welcome to HopeVoice Hub!

HopeVoice Hub is your community platform for sharing stories, accessing resources, and advocating for change. This guide will help you make the most of all the features available to you.

## 📱 Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform

1. Visit the HopeVoice Hub website (your deployment URL)
2. The site works on phones, tablets, and computers
3. No account required - start using immediately!
4. All your data stays on your device - complete privacy

### First Time Setup

When you first visit:
1. Read and accept the privacy notice
2. Adjust accessibility settings if needed (top-right corner)
3. Explore the different sections using the navigation menu

## 🎯 Core Features Guide

### 1. 🎙️ VoiceUp: Micro-Podcast Studio

**Purpose**: Share your story through voice recordings

**How to Use**:
1. Click **VoiceUp** in the navigation menu
2. Click **Start Recording** button
3. Speak for up to 2 minutes
4. Click **Stop Recording** when done
5. Listen to your recording
6. Add a title and select tags:
   - **Help Needed**: You need assistance
   - **Urgent**: Time-sensitive matter
   - **Daily Win**: Positive experience
   - **Challenge**: Difficulty you're facing
   - **Story**: General sharing
7. Choose **Post Anonymously** if you prefer
8. Click **Post Recording**

**Tips**:
- Find a quiet space for better audio quality
- The platform will automatically stop at 2 minutes
- You can discard and re-record if needed
- Browse the community feed to hear others' stories

### 2. 📋 Needs Board: Wish Wall

**Purpose**: Post urgent needs and find help

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to **Needs Board**
2. Fill out the form:
   - What do you need?
   - Additional details
   - Category (food, clothing, shelter, etc.)
   - Urgency level
   - Location (optional)
3. Choose to post anonymously if preferred
4. Click **Post Need**

**For Helpers**:
- Browse posted needs
- Click **I Can Help** to respond
- Leave a message about how you can assist

**Status Tracking**:
- **Open**: Need still exists
- **In Progress**: Someone is helping
- **Addressed**: Need has been met

### 3. 🗺️ Resource Radar

**Purpose**: Find services, shelters, meal sites, and resources

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **Resource Radar**
2. Use the search bar to find specific services
3. Filter by:
   - Category (shelter, food, medical, etc.)
   - Status (open now, closed)
4. View resource details:
   - Address and location
   - Phone number
   - Hours of operation
   - Description of services
5. Click **Get Directions** for navigation help
6. Click **Share** to share resource info with others

**Tip**: Bookmark frequently used resources

### 4. ⚖️ RightsBot: Automated Rights Support

**Purpose**: Get instant guidance on your legal rights

**How to Use**:
1. Visit **RightsBot**
2. Click on common topics or ask your own question
3. Read the guidance provided
4. Use the Incident Logger if you need to document a violation
5. The bot provides general guidance based on the Ontario Human Rights Code

**Common Topics**:
- Sleep disruption rights
- Shelter access rights
- Food access rights
- Discrimination protections
- Police interaction rights
- Eviction procedures

**Important**: This provides general guidance only. For specific legal advice, use "Ask a Lawyer" or contact a legal aid service.

### 5. 🏛️ Civic Stories: Council Submission

**Purpose**: Send messages directly to city council

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to **Civic Stories**
2. Fill out the submission form:
   - Subject line
   - Your message (max 500 words)
   - Category
3. Submit anonymously if desired
4. Click **Queue for Council**
5. Your submission is queued for review and delivery

**Tips**:
- Be clear and specific
- Focus on one issue per submission
- Explain how it affects you
- Suggest solutions if possible

### 6. 👨‍⚖️ Ask a Lawyer

**Purpose**: Get legal advice anonymously

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **Ask a Lawyer**
2. Select the topic category
3. Describe your legal question in detail
4. Submit your question
5. Check back for responses

**Topics Covered**:
- Housing & Tenancy
- Discrimination
- Human Rights
- Employment
- Other legal matters

### 7. 🍽️ FoodFinder

**Purpose**: Locate free meals and food resources

**How to Use**:
1. Visit **FoodFinder**
2. View upcoming meals with:
   - Location
   - Time
   - Days of week
3. Check the calendar for special events

**Tip**: Check regularly as schedules may change

### 8. 📅 Outreach Scheduler

**Purpose**: See when outreach teams will be in your area

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to **Outreach Scheduler**
2. View upcoming outreach visits:
   - Mobile health clinics
   - Outreach vans
   - Social workers
   - Faith group visits
3. Note dates, times, and locations

### 9. ✍️ Advocacy Letter Builder

**Purpose**: Create professional advocacy letters

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **Advocacy**
2. Select a template or start custom
3. Choose recipient (Council, Mayor, OHRC)
4. Write your message
5. Generate the letter
6. Download or copy for submission

**Templates Available**:
- Housing Rights
- Community Services
- Discrimination
- Custom Letter

### 10. 📝 Incident Logger

**Purpose**: Document rights violations for your records

**How to Use**:
1. Visit **Incident Logger**
2. Select incident type:
   - Sleep Deprivation
   - Denied Service
   - Discrimination
   - Harassment
   - Other
3. Describe what happened (be detailed):
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - People involved
   - What occurred
4. Click **Log Incident**

**Why Document**:
- Creates a record for potential legal action
- Helps identify patterns
- Supports advocacy efforts
- Provides evidence if needed

### 11. 📰 Street News Bulletin

**Purpose**: Stay informed about community news and events

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to **Street News**
2. Read featured articles and updates
3. Check for alerts and announcements
4. Look for upcoming events

### 12. ⭐ RespectPoint: Community Feedback

**Purpose**: Rate services and provide feedback

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **RespectPoint**
2. Enter the location/service name
3. Select a star rating (1-5 stars)
4. Add comments about your experience
5. Submit feedback

**Impact**: Your feedback helps improve services

### 13. 🎨 StreetGallery

**Purpose**: Share art, photography, and poetry

**How to Use**:
1. Visit **StreetGallery**
2. Upload your creative work
3. Add a title and description
4. Browse community artwork

**Tip**: All art forms welcome - share your creativity!

### 14. 🧭 Navigator: Peer Support

**Purpose**: Connect with experienced community mentors

**How to Use**:
1. Navigate to **Navigator**
2. Browse available mentors
3. View their experience and specialties
4. Click **Connect** to reach out

**Mentors Can Help With**:
- Navigating resources
- Housing support
- General advocacy
- Peer support

### 15. 💚 HealthLog: Wellbeing Tracker

**Purpose**: Track your daily health and wellbeing privately

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **HealthLog**
2. Daily entry form:
   - Hours of sleep
   - Stress level (1-10)
   - Overall feeling
   - Notes
3. Click **Save Entry**
4. View your log to see trends over time

**Privacy**: All health data stays on your device - completely private

### 16. 🗺️ HopeMap: Asset & Hazard Mapping

**Purpose**: Crowdsourced map of helpful resources and areas to avoid

**How to Use**:
1. Visit **HopeMap**
2. To add a pin:
   - Select type (Asset or Hazard)
   - Describe location
   - Add description
   - Submit
3. View all community pins
4. Use info to navigate safely and find resources

**Asset Examples**: Free WiFi, water fountain, safe rest area
**Hazard Examples**: Unsafe areas, broken services, known problems

## ⚙️ Accessibility Features

### High Contrast Mode
- Click the 🌗 button in the top-right
- Makes text easier to read
- Better for low vision users

### Font Size Controls
- Click A+ to increase font size
- Click A- to decrease font size
- Improves readability

### Reduced Motion
- Click the ⏸️ button
- Reduces animations
- Better for users sensitive to motion

### Keyboard Navigation
- Use Tab key to move between elements
- Use Enter to select
- Use arrow keys in menus

### Screen Reader Support
- All features work with screen readers
- ARIA labels provide context
- Announcements for important actions

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

### Your Data is Safe
- ✅ Everything stored on YOUR device only
- ✅ No external servers
- ✅ No tracking or analytics
- ✅ Post anonymously anytime
- ✅ Delete data whenever you want

### Managing Your Data

**Export Your Data**:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Click **Export All Data**
3. Save the file to your device
4. Keep as backup

**Delete Your Data**:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard
2. Click **Clear All Data**
3. Confirm deletion
4. All your data is removed

**Important**: If you clear browser data or use incognito mode, your information won't be saved.

## 💡 Tips for Best Experience

### General Tips
1. **Use Regularly**: The more you use it, the more helpful it becomes
2. **Be Descriptive**: Add details to help others understand
3. **Check Back Often**: New resources and updates are added
4. **Stay Anonymous**: Use anonymous posting for sensitive matters
5. **Export Data**: Backup important records regularly

### For Recording Voice
- Find a quiet space
- Speak clearly
- Hold device close to mouth
- Keep recordings under 2 minutes

### For Posting Needs
- Be specific about what you need
- Include urgency level
- Update when need is met
- Thank helpers publicly (encourages others)

### For Documenting Incidents
- Write immediately while fresh
- Include all details: date, time, location, people
- Be factual and specific
- Save evidence if possible
- Export your log regularly
